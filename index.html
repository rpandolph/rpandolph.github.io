Data Analysis & Coding

These are a mix of projects done both professionally and on my own time. The languages used are SQL, R, and Python.

R markdown

This script takes an excel file of monthly trade data and pulls out the data to update website text in English, Spanish, French and Portuguese. The script pulls data from an excel spreadsheet to replace the trade data figures, update the month and seasons referenced, and change references to the direction of change (increase or decrease).

R data visulization with geospatial data

Presentations & Articles

Coffee Market Report-December 2020

This report covers the coffee market (prices, supply, demand) with a focus on coffee trade for major consumer countries. All charts were made in Microsoft Excel, including its multi-level category chart to show imports and re-exports by type of coffee and country.

Coffee Market Report-November 2019

This is another monthly coffee market report but focuses on exports by type of coffee from coffee producing regions. Trade flows are shown in a sankey chart made in ggplot2

Coffee Development Report-Part III

The section on coffee market price volatility was done in collaboration with the International Food Policy Research Institute (IFPRI)
